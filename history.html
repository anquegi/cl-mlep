History of cl-mlep

cl-mlep was initially a study project of Frank Zalkow at the Institute for Musicology and Music computer sciences at University for Music Karlsruhe under supervision of Prof. Denis Lorrain. It's first ideas dated in November 2013. It evolved and was used in teaching Common Lisp and Machine Learning in summer term 2014.

Since January 2015 it's published on GitHub under MIT license and hopes to find users and contributers.
